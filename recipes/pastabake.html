Pasta Bake

Description

Enjoy this gooey cheese pasta bake for the ultimate weekday family dinner. Serve straight from the dish with a dressed green salad.

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion , finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves , crushed
  • ¼ tsp chilli flakes
  • 2 x 400g cans chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  • 6 tbsp mascarpone
  • 300g penne
  • 70g mature cheddar , grated
  • 50g grated mozzarella
  • ½ small bunch of parsley , finely chopped

Steps

  1. Heat 2 tbsp of the oil in a pan over a medium heat and fry the onion gently for 10-12 mins. Add the garlic and chilli flakes and cook for 1 min. Tip in the tomatoes and sugar and season to taste. Simmer uncovered for 20 mins or until thickened, then stir through the mascarpone.
  2. Heat the oven to 220C/200C fan/gas 7. Cook the penne following pack instructions. Drain and toss with the remaining oil. Tip the pasta into a medium sized ovenproof dish. Stir in the sauce. Top with the cheddar, mozzarella and parsley. Bake for 20 mins or until golden brown and bubbling.
  3. Enjoy!

Chicken Balti

Description

Spiced chicken balti, this Indian takeaway classic is characteristically a meat (or veg) dish with a thick, spicy tomato-based sauce. Usually, it's heavy on the chilli heat with a little tanginess from the yoghurt that's added.

Ingredients

  1. 1 tbsp sunflower oil
  2. 2 large onions , thickly sliced
  3. 4 skinless chicken breasts
  4. 4 tbsp balti curry paste
  5. 200g quinoa
  6. 400g can chopped tomato
  7. 1l chicken stock
  8. 50g roasted salted cashew
  9. small bunch coriander , leaves chopped
